# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Thai EV buyers, tech enthusiasts, and eco-conscious drivers. They use the platform to research electric vehicle options, compare technical specifications side-by-side, and calculate real-world financial savings (electricity costs vs petrol, charging times, etc.), often while browsing on mobile devices at dealerships or at home.

## Product Purpose
EV Compare Thailand is a high-utility, precise web application that empowers prospective buyers to make informed decisions when purchasing an electric vehicle. Success means users can easily find, compare, and simulate EV usage scenarios (charging times, cost savings) with absolute confidence in the accuracy and readability of the data.

## Brand Personality
Informative, precise, and tech-forward. The tone is expert and reliable, ensuring that density of data is balanced with excellent legibility and layout organization.

## Anti-references
- Cliché SaaS glassmorphism, decorative blurs, and floating cards.
- Insanely rounded corners on cards, badges, and buttons (>12px).
- Excessive neon gradients, text gradients, or diagonal stripe backgrounds that distract from reading data tables and charts.
- Aggressive, gamified visual accents.

## Design Principles
1. **Utility-First Information Design**: Design exists to make data readable. Comparison tables, spec sheets, and calculator outputs take visual priority over decorative elements.
2. **Honest and Uncluttered Layouts**: Use clean grids and structured typography instead of visual fluff. Shadows are subtle or omitted in favor of solid, fine borders.
3. **Structured Complexity**: Group dense technical specs (battery, charging, performance) logically to reduce cognitive load while preserving detailed technical information.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict compliance with WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text).
- Seamless mobile-first design, as many users browse specs on-site at dealerships.
- Distraction-free interactions, respecting user reduced-motion preferences.
